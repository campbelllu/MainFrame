Good Value Tool

Judging whether a stock is currently expensive or cheap is based upon past performance, and current financial health.

This area of the site will judge a stock based on its dividend per share versus price per share, giving users a simple valuation ratio to understand what they're paying for what they're receiving each year.

While not a commonly used valuation metric, it can be useful to find stocks on sale, especially when considering how many dollars to spend per one dollar put in your pocket via corporate distributions.
